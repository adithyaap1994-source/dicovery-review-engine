# Research Plan

## Target Segment

Comfort Loopers with Discovery Intent.

These are frequent Spotify users who rely on familiar playlists but still want controlled discovery.

## Screening Criteria

- Uses Spotify at least 4 days per week
- Often replays the same playlists, artists, or songs
- Feels recommendations can be repetitive or stale
- Wants to discover new music without spending much effort
- Usually chooses music based on mood, activity, or vibe

## Interview Questions

1. Walk me through how you decide what to play.
2. When do you want to discover new music?
3. What makes a recommendation feel good?
4. When does Spotify get discovery wrong?
5. Why do you return to familiar songs or playlists?
6. What do you wish Spotify understood better about your current mood or intent?
7. Would you use an AI discovery assistant that lets you control novelty level?

## Validation Goals

- Confirm whether repetition is caused by comfort, poor recommendations, lack of control, or discovery effort.
- Identify what "fresh but not random" means to users.
- Learn which MVP controls feel useful versus gimmicky.
